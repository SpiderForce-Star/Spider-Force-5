# Spider-Force 5

**Cockpit Combat Simulator** — A pure HTML5 Canvas space combat game.

Sit in the cockpit of your ship, watch the radar, line up the crosshair, and take on five increasingly difficult challenges against alien invaders.

## Play Online (GitHub Pages)

1. Go to this repository’s **Settings** → **Pages**
2. Under **Build and deployment**:
   - Source: **Deploy from a branch**
   - Branch: **main**
   - Folder: **/ (root)**
3. Click **Save**
4. Wait 30–60 seconds, then visit:  
   **https://spiderforce-star.github.io/Spider-Force-5/**

## Play Locally

Just open `index.html` in any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari).

## Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| **WASD** or **Arrow Keys** | Pitch & Yaw (Up / Down / Left / Right) |
| **Shift** (hold while turning) | Bank the ship |
| **Shift** (tap while turning) | Full barrel roll |
| **Space** | Fire twin blue lasers |
| **Enter** | Fire a missile **or** detonate the active missile |
| **Esc** | Pause / Resume |

You receive **5 missiles per challenge**. Fire one, then press Enter again at the right distance to detonate it and destroy multiple enemy ships at once.

## Challenges

1. First Contact  
2. Swarm Rising  
3. Heavy Assault  
4. Elite Wing  
5. Spider’s Final Stand  

Difficulty increases with each challenge (more enemies, faster ships, tougher hulls, higher fire rates).

## Features

- First-person cockpit view with panels, switches, gauges, and crosshair
- Live radar that accurately shows approaching threats
- Twin blue lasers + detonatable missiles
- Alien red laser fire
- Progressive 5-challenge campaign
- Hull integrity, score, and real-time messages

Built with pure HTML5 + Canvas — no frameworks, no installs.

Enjoy the fight, Pilot.
